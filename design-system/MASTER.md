# QingLue AI Consulting Design System

## Positioning
- Brand: QingLue AI Consulting / QingLue.
- Promise: AI-powered insight, strategic reconstruction, measurable value growth.
- Audience: business owners, executives, strategy leads, transformation leaders.
- Tone: precise, executive, intelligent, confident, practical.

## Visual Direction
- Concept: "Strategic intelligence cockpit" rather than generic technology blueprints.
- First impression: calm authority with an interactive AI operations field.
- Avoid copying the supplied poster's sky-city illustration style.
- Avoid one-note blue palettes; pair deep ink, warm ivory, cobalt, teal, and amber.

## Color Tokens
- `--ink`: #07111f
- `--ink-2`: #0d1b2f
- `--paper`: #f7f5ef
- `--surface`: #ffffff
- `--line`: #d9dee8
- `--text`: #102033
- `--muted`: #667085
- `--blue`: #2157ff
- `--teal`: #19b7a2
- `--amber`: #f0a638
- `--coral`: #ec6f62

## Typography
- Chinese and Latin system stack for fast loading and professional enterprise feel.
- Hero H1 should be strong but not oversized on mobile.
- Body text minimum 16px, line-height around 1.65.
- Use tabular figures for metrics and calculator output.

## Layout
- Mobile-first, no horizontal scrolling.
- Sticky navigation with clear current/action states.
- First viewport must reveal brand, value proposition, primary actions, and interactive visual.
- Sections use full-width bands and constrained inner content, not nested cards.
- Cards only for repeated service/case/process units.

## Interaction
- Hero canvas responds to pointer movement and scroll.
- Buttons have visible pressed/focus states.
- Service cards reveal detail on hover/focus/click.
- Diagnostic sliders calculate score and recommendation live.
- Use tabs for solution views and clickable process steps.
- Contact form validates required inputs and shows success feedback.

## Accessibility
- All controls use labels or aria-labels.
- Interactive targets are at least 44px tall.
- Support reduced-motion by pausing canvas/animations where practical.
- Maintain contrast for text on dark and light surfaces.
